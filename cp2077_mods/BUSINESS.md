# Grand Theft Cyberpunk - Business Strategy & Models

## 1. Game Studio Partnership Model
Our partnership model aims to integrate Metaconscious Singularity Node (MSN) architecture directly into AAA and indie titles.
- **Revenue Share:** A percentage split of revenue generated through in-game purchases tied to AI features, such as custom skill acquisitions or advanced AI companion modules.
- **Co-Marketing Agreements:** Joint marketing campaigns highlighting the cutting-edge "Sovereign AI" features powered by MSN.
- **Tiered Integration Support:** Ranging from basic API access to full on-site engineering support for deep narrative integration.

## 2. Narrative AI Licensing Terms
Licensing the Lyra Dialogue System and MSN core for dynamic narrative generation.
- **Indie Tier:** Affordable monthly subscription + small royalty percentage. Ideal for studios with < 50 employees.
- **AAA Tier:** Custom enterprise pricing, perpetual licensing options for specific titles, and dedicated support channels. Includes rights to train custom models on studio-owned lore.
- **Data Privacy & IP:** Studios retain full rights to their generated lore and characters. Core MSN learning models do not absorb proprietary studio data without explicit consent.

## 3. Quest Generation SaaS
A cloud-based SaaS platform providing dynamic, infinite quest generation tailored to a game's world state and player choices.
- **Pay-per-API-Call:** For live service games where quests are generated on the fly.
- **Batch Generation Licensing:** For single-player games looking to populate their world pre-launch, billed per thousands of quests generated.
- **Features:** Includes character consistency checks, lore-accurate generation, and multi-branching narrative support.

## 4. Revenue Streams (Projected)
- **Skill Marketplace:** Direct-to-consumer sales of custom MSN skills.
  - *Current Valuation:* 20 skills × $3.50 average price = $70.00 baseline projection per user. Scaled across a target user base of 100k, this represents a significant initial revenue stream.
- **PAC Pre-Launch:** Pre-launch access campaigns and early adopter bundles for the Premium AI Companion (PAC) tier.
- **Quest-Gen Licensing:** Ongoing SaaS revenue from B2B game studio partnerships.
